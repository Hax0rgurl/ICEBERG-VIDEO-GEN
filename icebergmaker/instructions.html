How to Use Iceberg Maker

Important: Patience Required! ⏳

⚠️ Regarding Generation Times

This tool generates unique text and images for every single entry on the iceberg. This process takes time!

  • 3-5 Levels: Recommended for best speed.
  • 10 Levels: Will take a significantly long time. Please be PATIENT and WAIT. It works fine, but generating 30+ unique AI images takes time.
  • 99 Levels: This will effectively take FOREVER. Not recommended unless you plan to leave the tab open for hours.

Do not close the tab while the progress bar is moving. It is working!

🧊 Creating an Iceberg

  1. Choose a Topic Type:
    • Custom: For general topics (History, Science, Pop Culture).
    • Mystery: Adds a spooky/conspiracy theme to the levels.
    • Danger: Uses warning levels (Safe to Deadly).
  2. Set Levels: Choose how deep the iceberg goes. Remember the warning above!
  3. Enter Topic: Type what you want the iceberg to be about.
  4. Generate: Click and wait for the magic to happen.

💾 Downloading

Clicking "Download All" will create a ZIP file containing:

  • A detailed text file with descriptions, significance, and controversy for every entry.
  • High-quality images for every entry on the iceberg.
